SECTION 13. CONSENT AND NOTIFICATION
(1) An organization shall not collect, use or disclose personal data about an individual unless:
(a) the individual gives, or is deemed to have given, his consent under this Act to the collection, use or disclosure; or
(b) the collection, use or disclosure, as the case may be without the consent of the individual is required or authorised under this Act or any other written law.

(2) An organization must ensure that any personal data collected is accurate and complete.

(3) Ideally, the organization should monitor internal processes regularly.
